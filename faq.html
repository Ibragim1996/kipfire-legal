Q: What's included in the Free tier?
  • 5 AI-generated tasks per day (resets every 24 hours)
  • Access to all 6 categories
  • Video recording and editing tools
  • KIPFIRE watermark on videos
Q: What's included in Premium?
  • Unlimited AI-generated tasks (24/7 access, no daily limits)
  • All Free tier features included
  • Access to all future premium features at no additional cost
  • Priority support
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
For iOS: Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí KIPFIRE ‚Üí Cancel Subscription

For Android: Google Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí KIPFIRE ‚Üí Cancel Subscription

Q: Do I get a refund if I cancel?
Refunds are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android), not by KIPFIRE:

iOS: You can request a refund within 14 days by contacting Apple Support at reportaproblem.apple.com

Android: You can request a refund within 48 hours via Google Play
If you cancel without requesting a refund: You keep Premium access until the end of your paid period. We do not provide partial refunds for unused time.
Q: When will I be charged?
Monthly: Charged every month on your subscription date
Annual: Charged every year on your subscription date

You can cancel anytime to prevent future charges. Canceling does not trigger an immediate charge or refund‚Äîyou simply keep access until your current period ends.

üîí Privacy & Security

Q: What data do you collect?
We collect only:
  • Your email address (for account login)
  • Device language (to provide content in your language)
  • Number of tasks generated (to enforce daily limits)
We do NOT collect: Videos, location data, phone numbers, names, or browsing history.
Q: Where are my videos stored?
All videos are stored ONLY on your device. We never upload, access, or store your videos on our servers.
Your videos are saved directly to your device's photo gallery, and you have complete control over them.
Q: Do you sell my data?
NO. NEVER. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
Q: Is my data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard security measures:
  • Firebase Authentication (Google) for secure login
  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • GDPR and CCPA compliance
  • Regular security updates
For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
